Feature Extraction Pipeline

Our system extracts 191 features from each audio sample using the following pipeline:

MFCC (40 coefficients)

Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficients

Chroma (12 bins)

Chroma Energy Normalized

Mel Spectrogram (20 bins)

Mel-frequency power spectrogram

Spectral Contrast (7 bands)

Spectral contrast features

Zero Crossing Rate

Rate of sign changes in signal

RMS Energy

Root Mean Square energy

Spectral Centroid

Center of spectral mass

Spectral Rolloff

Frequency below which 85% energy lies
