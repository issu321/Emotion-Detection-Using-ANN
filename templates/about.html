How It Works

Our end-to-end pipeline processes audio through several sophisticated stages:

1

Audio Collection

Users upload audio files (WAV, MP3, OGG, M4A, FLAC, WEBM) through our intuitive web interface. The system accepts recordings up to 32MB in size.

2

Signal Preprocessing

Audio signals are normalized, resampled to a standard rate, and segmented into 3-second clips for consistent analysis.

3

Feature Extraction

We extract 191 features including 40 MFCCs, 12 chroma features, 128 mel spectrogram bins, spectral contrast, ZCR, RMS energy, and spectral centroid using Librosa.

4

ANN Classification

The Artificial Neural Network processes extracted features through 4 hidden layers (256, 128, 64, 32 neurons) with ReLU activation, batch normalization, dropout, and softmax output for 7 emotion classes.

5

Real-Time Dashboard

Results are displayed instantly with confidence scores, probability distributions, waveform visualization, and stored in your personal history for trend analysis.

Emotion Classes

Our ANN model is trained to recognize seven fundamental human emotions from speech:

Neutral

Calm, balanced tone

Happy

Joyful, upbeat speech

Sad

Melancholic, low energy

Angry

Intense, harsh tone

Fearful

Anxious, trembling voice

Disgusted

Surprised
