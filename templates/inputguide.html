Pro Tip

WAV and FLAC formats provide the best quality for emotion analysis since they are uncompressed or losslessly compressed. Avoid heavily compressed MP3s below 128kbps.

Recording Guidelines

1

Quiet Environment

Record in a quiet room with minimal background noise. Background music, traffic, or conversations can interfere with emotion detection accuracy.

2

Optimal Distance

Keep the microphone 6-12 inches (15-30 cm) from your mouth. Too close may cause clipping; too far reduces signal quality.

3

Speak Naturally

Speak in your natural voice at a comfortable volume. The system is designed to detect subtle emotional cues in normal speech patterns.

4

Duration

Audio clips should be at least 3 seconds long for accurate feature extraction. The system analyzes the first 3 seconds of each file.

Important

Maximum file size is 32MB. For batch uploads, you can process up to 10 files simultaneously. Files longer than 3 seconds will be automatically trimmed from the beginning.

Audio Quality Tips

  • Use a sample rate of 22050 Hz or higher for best results
  • Mono (single channel) audio is preferred over stereo
  • 16-bit depth is sufficient for emotion analysis
  • Avoid audio with heavy compression artifacts
  • Ensure consistent volume levels throughout the recording
  • Remove silence at the beginning and end of recordings

Understanding Results

After analysis, you'll see:

  • Predicted Emotion: The most likely emotion detected
  • Confidence Score: Percentage indicating model certainty (higher is better)
  • Probability Distribution: Likelihood of all 7 emotions
  • Waveform: Visual representation of the audio signal
  • Top 3 Emotions: The three most likely emotions ranked

Confidence Threshold

Confidence scores above 70% indicate high reliability. Scores between 50-70% suggest mixed emotions. Below 50% may indicate unclear audio or neutral emotional state.

Troubleshooting

  • Upload fails: Check file size (max 32MB) and format compatibility
  • Low confidence: Ensure clear audio with minimal background noise
  • Wrong emotion: Use the feedback button to help improve the model
  • Recording issues: Allow microphone permissions in your browser
  • Batch errors: Ensure all files are valid audio formats
