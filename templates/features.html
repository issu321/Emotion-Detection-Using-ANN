Real-Time Emotion Detection

Upload any audio file and get instant emotion analysis. Our ANN model processes audio in under 1 second, delivering accurate predictions with confidence scores for all 7 emotion classes.

  • Instant processing under 1 second
  • Support for WAV, MP3, OGG, M4A, FLAC, WEBM

Advanced Audio Feature Extraction

Our system extracts 191 acoustic features using Librosa, including 40 MFCCs, 12 chroma features, 128 mel spectrogram bins, spectral contrast, and temporal features for comprehensive analysis.

  • 40 MFCC coefficients
  • Chroma energy features
  • Mel-frequency spectrogram
  • Spectral contrast analysis
  • Zero-crossing rate & RMS energy
  • Spectral centroid & rolloff

Interactive Dashboard

Visualize your emotion analysis results with beautiful, interactive charts. Track your emotional patterns over time with detailed statistics, trend analysis, and export capabilities.

  • Real-time probability charts

  • Export to CSV/JSON
